About us

Welcome to Renova CRM, a comprehensive customer relationship management solution designed to streamline your business operations. Whether you're on the go or at your desk, Renova CRM ensures you have full control of your customer interactions and service management.

At Renova CRM, we believe in making CRM intuitive and powerful. Our mission is to help businesses like yours grow by providing the tools needed to manage customer relationships effectively, drive sales, and deliver top-notch service. Join us and transform the way you manage your business today.

Our platform is tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses, offering a seamless experience across both mobile and desktop devices. Renova CRM is built with three powerful modules:

  1. Admin Module: Manage customer and technician data effortlessly. With the ability to assign technicians and tokens for complaint resolutions, add new sales orders, and generate detailed receipt reports, the Admin Module puts everything you need at your fingertips.
  2. Customer Module: Our customer-centric tools empower users to add complaints directly, track the resolution process by technicians, add sales orders, and view detailed receipts for their records. This ensures a transparent and efficient service experience.
  3. Technician Module: Our technician tools are designed to enhance service efficiency. Technicians can easily update the status of complaints, mark them as resolved, and submit receipts for completed work. This ensures that every job is tracked and documented for future reference.
